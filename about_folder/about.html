About Ournimation

Ournimation is a dedicated home for 2D animation artists to share their work, educate one another, and learn from fellow animators. It is a focused community where creators can trade feedback, explore new techniques, and grow together through the art of 2D animation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a supportive community space for growth in 2D animation, helping artists develop their skills and become confident, talented animators through shared knowledge and encouragement.

  • Learn animation.
  • Engage with posts.
  • Become a popular animator.
